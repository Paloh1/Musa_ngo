About Us

Musa Community Development & Sustainability Organization (MCDSO) is a youth led non-profit, non-governmental organization dedicated to promoting environmental conservation, fighting climate change, advocating for climate and empowering communities with sustainable livelihood skills.

Our mission is to foster eco-friendly practices, strengthen local capacities, and encourage collective responsibility for a greener and more sustainable future.

Our Services

Community Empowerment

We conduct training and workshops that help local communities develop sustainable income-generating activities.

Environmental Education

We raise awareness on environmental protection and promote eco-friendly lifestyles through campaigns and youth programs.

Climate Action Initiatives

We support tree planting, waste management, and renewable energy projects to reduce climate impact.

Peace Building

We support communities affected by conflict in their journey toward peace, reconciliation, and justice. Our peace-building initiatives focus on dialogue, mediation, and healing, while promoting transitional justice mechanisms that ensure fairness, truth, and long-term stability

Partners

Join us in building resilient communities and protecting our planet. We are sponsored by three partners in our oganisation .

United for Climate Justice

We are climate justice activists on the frontlines working together across the globe to bring about a more just and sustainable world. Climate is the biological basis of life and our starting point as we work towards a fairer and more democratic future.

Stay Grounded

Stay Grounded is a global network of more than 200 member organisations Among them local airport opposition and climate justice groups, NGOs, trade unions, initiatives fostering alternatives to aviation like night trains, and organisations supporting communities which struggle against offset or so called SAF (Sustainable Aviation Fuels) projects. Also individual activists, academics, trade unionists and interested people can contribute to the network

Youth 4 Nature

At Youth4Nature , we stand firm on the critical role youth play in influencing decision-making and implementation frameworks. By uplifting youth's ambition and moral authority, we can influence key climate-nature decisions to determine and shape our collective futures.
